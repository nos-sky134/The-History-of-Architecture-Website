What are different styles of architecture?


Architecture is the planning and designing of large man-made objects such as buildings, bridges or public spaces. Over time, different styles of building designs from different parts of the world were developed to fit the needs of society. Styles slowly develop over a long period of time and will slowly fall out of favor due to societal changes. The dates listed in these next few lessons only mark when they were the most popular.

History of Architecture

by Emily Chen And Katie Chen

An educational website aimed at teaching architectural history to youths between the ages of 13-18. 
Site includes articles written for 5 different eras of architectural styles as well as a quiz for each article.
Made using HTML/CSS/and JavaScript.
